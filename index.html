Why Peak Roofing

We Sign Our Name
To Every Roof We Lay.

Steve started Peak Roofing in 2005 from a single van in Bradford. He had one rule: if he wouldn't put it on his own house, he wouldn't put it on yours. Twenty years later, that same rule runs through everything we do — from a two-tile repair in Ilkley to a full roof replacement on a Halifax terrace.

Every job gets a written guarantee. Not a verbal promise. Not small print. A proper document with our name and number on it.
